About us

Sapphire Academy was established on the 10th of August, 1999
in the city of Lagos by Dr. Mrs. Ekwe Alabi. It was founded
out of passion for Quality and Resourceful Education.
It is the first Tech-Based School in Lagos, Nigeria
and it's renowned for its outstanding
Curriculum and Efficient Practical.

Mission Statement

To provide Tech-Based Quality Education which helps our Students
to discover their Potentials and do well within and outside
the Academic walls with the help of experienced
and passionate Teachers.
